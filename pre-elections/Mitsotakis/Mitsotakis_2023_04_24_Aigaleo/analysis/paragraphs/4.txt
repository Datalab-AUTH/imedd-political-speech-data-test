In 2019 we said we would cut taxes for everyone. Today Greeks are exempt from fifty taxes and contributions. You are all paying 35% lower ENVAT, more than the 30% I promised you. The tax rate on individuals fell from 22% to 9%. The corporate rate for all businesses, small and medium-sized businesses, here in the region, has dropped from 29% to 22%. The solidarity levy and the parental benefit tax were finally abolished.
