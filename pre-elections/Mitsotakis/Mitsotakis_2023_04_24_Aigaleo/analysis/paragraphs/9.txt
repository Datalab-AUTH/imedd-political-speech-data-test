Count how many crises we have been through together: migratory invasions in the Evros and the Aegean, the global coronavirus pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the energy "boom", natural disasters, the daily threats of Turkey. No government since the post-war period has faced so many obstacles in such a short time. Yet this government has kept our borders impregnable. With a gigantic programme it supported workers in business and work. It organised an exemplary vaccination campaign, at the same time implementing the commitments for which the Greek people had entrusted it.
