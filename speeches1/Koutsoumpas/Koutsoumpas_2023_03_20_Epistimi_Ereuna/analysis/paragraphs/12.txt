	So how to address the "skills gap" in the market system.  But, in market terms, i.e. supply and demand for qualifications - skills and not labour, no longer.  Therefore, Universities now have to produce "qualified people" of various categories and speeds.  This means that Curricula should be numerous, flexible, as free as possible in form and developed around learning objectives related to qualifications.  Their duration, which is also flexible, is organised on the basis of cycles of study measured in credits.
