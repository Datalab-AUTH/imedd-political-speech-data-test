 When designing and implementing an infrastructure project, future social needs and potential will be taken into account, all measures for overall savings will be taken.  	This enormous potential of the Socialist system is one of the aspects of its superiority over the capitalist system.  	For an example of what a capitalist system means, we need only cite a typical example: Listen to this: a bridge worth billions was built, to which no railway infrastructure was added, which would have cost only 5% more.  Not because of a mistake - watch out - but because the group that manages the bridge wanted to "torpedo" competition with other road transport groups in perpetuity.
