 Tens of thousands of illegal immigrants, transported en masse from all parts of Turkey, constantly attempted - and often with the use of violence - to enter Greek territory. And already by the end of February, from that night of the 28th to the 29th of February, the place, not here but mainly further north, in Kastanies, had essentially turned into a battlefield, with attempts to invade accompanied by operations to undermine and destroy the deterrent barrier that exists in northern Evros on the other side of the border.  What we experienced here, three years ago, was the first but not the last phase of a new form of hybrid warfare breaking out in Europe.
